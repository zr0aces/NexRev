# NexRev — Daily Usage Guide

A practical, step-by-step guide to using NexRev as your daily sales operating system.

---

## Your Daily Routine

### ☀️ Morning: Plan Your Day
1. **Check Today Tab**: Review your pending follow-ups in the 3-column card grid.
2. **Urgency Check**: Cards with red dots are **Overdue**, amber are **Due Soon**.
3. **Navigate**: Click any card to jump directly to that opportunity in the **Pipeline** view.
4. **Review Metrics**: Check the top row cards for a quick health check (Total Value, Overdue Count, etc.).

### 🏃 During the Day: Log & Automate
1. **Log Notes**: Paste raw meeting or call notes into the text area.
2. **AI Tasks**: Click **📋 Extract tasks** to let the AI automatically populate your Kanban board with action items from your notes.
3. **AI Summary**: Use **✨ AI summarize** for a clean, bulleted version of messy notes.
4. **CRM Sync**: Click **▪ SF update note** to generate a note ready for Salesforce. This strictly includes only activities since your last sync.

---

## Managing Opportunities

### Adding a New Account
- Click **+ Add Opportunity** (or `Ctrl+K`).
- **Account Search**: Type the account name. If it exists, select it to go straight to the Pipeline view for that account.
- **One Record Rule**: You can only have one active opportunity per client. The system will block duplicates.
- **Next Follow-up**: Always set a follow-up date to ensure it appears in your Today view.

### Pipeline Management
- **Search & Filter**: Use the top bar to filter by stage or search for keywords.
- **Kanban Board**: Drag and drop cards between **To Do**, **Follow-ups**, and **Done**.
- **Stage Updates**: Update the deal stage as it progresses from Prospecting to Closed Won.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Keep the "Today" Clean**: Always update follow-up dates or move tasks to "Done" to clear your daily view.
2. **Log Raw, Polish with AI**: Don't waste time formatting notes during meetings. Paste them raw and use the AI tools to clean them up later.
3. **Weekly Backups**: Use the **Backup** button in the nav once a week to keep a JSON snapshot of your data.

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- `Ctrl + K`: Add New Opportunity.
- `Esc`: Close Modals / Cancel search.
- `Enter` (in Kanban): Quick add card.
