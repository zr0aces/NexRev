# NexRev User Guide

Welcome to NexRev! This guide will help you get started with managing your sales pipeline efficiently.

---

## 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: Use your credentials to sign in. The default is `admin` / `admin`.
- **Navigation**: Use the tabs at the top to switch between **Today**, **Pipeline**, and **Activity Log**.

## 2. Profile & Telegram Notifications
- **Profile Tab**: Navigate to the **Profile** tab to manage your account.
- **Security**: You can update your password in the Security section.
- **Telegram Setup**: Click **🔗 Link Automatically** to open Telegram and connect your account instantly. Alternatively, you can still enter your Chat ID manually if preferred.
- **Daily Digest**: Every morning at **8:30 AM**, you will receive a summary of deals due today and overdue items directly on Telegram.

## 3. Managing Your Day (Today Tab)
- **Daily Dashboard**: View your urgent follow-ups in a 3-column card grid.
- **Urgency Indicators**: 
    - 🔴 **Red**: Overdue follow-up.
    - 🟡 **Amber**: Due today or within 3 days.
- **Quick Jump**: Click any card to instantly navigate to that account in the Pipeline view.

## 3. Working with Opportunities (Pipeline Tab)
- **Account Search**: Use the search bar to find specific clients or keywords.
- **Stage Filters**: Click on stage badges (e.g., Prospecting, Negotiation) to filter your list.
- **One Record Policy**: Each client can only have one active opportunity. If you search for an existing account while trying to add a new one, the system will redirect you to the existing record.

## 4. Automation & AI Tools
- **Logging Notes**: Enter notes in the detail panel and click **Log Note**.
- **Extracting Tasks**: Click **📋 Extract tasks** to let AI scan your notes and create Kanban cards for you.
- **SF Sync**: Click **▪ SF update note** to get a summary ready to paste into Salesforce. It only covers what’s happened since your last sync.
- **AI Summary**: Use **✨ AI summarize** to turn messy meeting notes into professional bullet points.

## 5. Kanban Board
- **To Do**: Tasks you need to start.
- **Follow-ups**: Items where you are waiting for a response.
- **Done**: Completed tasks.
- **Drag & Drop**: Simply drag cards between columns to update their status.

## 6. Tips for Success
- **Always set a follow-up date**: This ensures the account shows up in your **Today** view.
- **Regular Backups**: Use the **Backup** button in the top menu once a week.
- **Privacy**: Your data is stored locally on your server. No one else has access to it.
