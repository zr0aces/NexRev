NexRev Master Guide

The definitive source for managing your sales pipeline, daily routines, and AI integrations.

1. Getting Started

  • Login: Sign in with your credentials (Default: admin / admin).
  • Desktop Navigation: Switch between Today, Pipeline, Activity Log, and Profile.
  • Mobile Navigation: Use the Bottom Nav and the Floating (+) Button for speed.

2. Your Daily Routine

  • Plan (Morning): Review your Today dashboard. Red cards are overdue; Amber are due soon.
  • Execute: Log meeting notes directly in the activity area. Paste them raw; let AI clean them up.
  • Automate: Use 📋 Extract tasks to instantly populate your Kanban board from your notes.
  • Sync: Generate ▪ SF update notes to quickly update your CRM with the latest developments.

3. Profile & Telegram Notifications

  • Telegram Setup: Link your account via the Profile tab to receive daily digests at 8:30 AM.
  • Security: Change your password immediately to keep your private pipeline data secure.

4. Pipeline & Kanban

  • Search: Find any account instantly. Note: One client = One opportunity.
  • Kanban: Track micro-tasks across To Do → Follow-ups → Done.
  • Master-Detail (Mobile): Tap an account for details; tap "Back" to return to the list.

5. AI Productivity Tools

  • ✨ AI Summarize: Turn messy notes into professional bullet points.
  • 📋 Task Extraction: Scan logs for action items and create Kanban cards automatically.

6. Keyboard Shortcuts (Desktop)

  • Ctrl + K : Add New Opportunity
  • Esc : Close Modals / Cancel Search
  • Enter : Quick-add card in Kanban input

7. Best Practices

  • Clean Slate: Always update follow-up dates to keep your Today view focused on current tasks.
  • SQLite-First: Your data is stored in a single local SQLite database. Backup is as simple as copying the data/nexrev.sqlite3 file.
